About Discretopia

Last Updated: 12 January 2023

In response to the sparse availability of online resources dedicated to discrete mathematics, Discretopia was created. Its mission is to serve as a unified, well-organized, and friendly guide to the essential topics covered in a college-level discrete mathematics course.

You'll notice that Discretopia has a familiar, highly-linked structure. Related concepts are no more than a few clicks away. The strength in this approach is that when learning about something new, readers will find that if they need a primer on a related topic, a relevant link will always be at the ready. This is important because callbacks to previous material happen a lot in discrete math. Another benefit is that the more "hardcore" of the readers will have every opportunity to go down a discrete math rabbit hole and satisfy their curiosity.

Discretopia takes pride in its connectedness. This is why the website's logo is a \(K_8\), a complete graph with beautiful symmetry!
